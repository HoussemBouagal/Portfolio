üß† Hybrid Model Architecture

Collaborative Filtering

  • Learns user-movie interactions from ratings
  • 50-dimensional embeddings for users and movies
  • Captures patterns in user preferences
  • "Users who liked X also liked Y" approach

Content-Based Filtering

  • Analyzes movie features and genres
  • Multi-label genre encoding (One-hot)
  • "Movies similar to what you watched"
  • Genre-based similarity matching

Combined Hybrid Approach:

The model combines both approaches to overcome limitations of each method. Collaborative filtering handles user preferences while content-based filtering ensures recommendations stay within preferred genres, resulting in more accurate and diverse suggestions.
